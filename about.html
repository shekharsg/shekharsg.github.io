Brief Biography

I am Shekhar Sharan Goyal, a Research Scientist at the Helmholtz Centre for Environmental Research (UFZ), Leipzig, Germany. I specialize in agricultural nutrient management, ecohydrology, and sustainable trade networks. My current research focuses on stream-temperature modeling under the FluSeeQ project, aiming to forecast hydrological stress across Germany.

Education

    Dissertation: Quantifying Nutrient Pollution in Agricultural Systems, Restructuring Strategies for Management, and Assessing Implications on Trade Networks

  • M.Sc. Geology (Hons), Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (2016–2018)

Research Experience
