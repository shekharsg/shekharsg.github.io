AP sees six-fold rise in agri nitrogen surplus since 1966
Published: February 9, 2025

Between 1966 and 2017, Andhra Pradesh experienced a notable increase in agricultural nitrogen surplus, with a mean of 111.74 kg/ha. The highest recorded surplus was 198.8 (kg/ha) in 2013, and the lowest was 29.3 (kg/ha) in 1966, showing an almost six-fold increase over 50 years. This growing surplus reflects intensified fertiliser usage, driven by the Green Revolution and expanding agricultural practices.
